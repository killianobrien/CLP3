Evaluating Fourier series at particular points can generate interesting series formulae. For instance, use evaluation at \(x=\frac{\pi}{2}\) of the Fourier series for the square wave to obtain the following formula for \(\pi\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \pi = 4 \sum_{m=1}^\infty \frac{(-1)^{m+1}}{2m -1} . \end{equation*}
