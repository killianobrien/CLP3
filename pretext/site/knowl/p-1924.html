The surface \(x^4+y^4+z^4+xyz=17\) passes through \((0,1,2)\text{,}\) and near this point the surface determines \(x\) as a function, \(x=F(y,z)\text{,}\) of \(y\) and \(z\text{.}\)

  1. Find \(F_y\) and \(F_z\) at \((x,y,z)=(0,1,2)\text{.}\)
  2. Use the tangent plane approximation (also known as linear, first order or differential approximation) to find the approximate value of \(x\) (near \(0\)) such that \((x,1.01, 1.98)\) lies on the surface.
