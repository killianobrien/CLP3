The solid \(E\) is bounded below by the paraboloid \(z = x^2 + y^2\) and above by the cone \(z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\text{.}\) Let

\begin{equation*} I = \tripInt_E z\big(x^2+y^2+z^2\big)\ \dee{V} \end{equation*}
  1. Write \(I\) in terms of cylindrical coordinates. Do not evaluate.
  2. Write \(I\) in terms of spherical coordinates. Do not evaluate.
  3. Calculate \(I\text{.}\)
