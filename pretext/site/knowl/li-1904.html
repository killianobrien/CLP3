The surface \(x = 2-y-z\text{,}\) or equivalenty, \(x+y+z=2\) is a plane. It passes through the points \((2,0,0)\text{,}\) \((0,2,0)\) and \((0,0,2)\text{.}\) It is sketched in the bottom figure below. We know that our domain of integration extends to \(y=-1\text{,}\) so we have chosen to include in the sketch the part of the plane in \(x\ge 0\text{,}\) \(y\ge-1\text{,}\) \(z\ge 0\text{.}\)
