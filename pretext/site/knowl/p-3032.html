(a) On the domain of integration

  • \(y\) runs from \(0\) to \(4\) and
  • for each \(y\) in the range \(0\le y\le 1\text{,}\) \(x\) runs from \(-\sqrt{y}\) to \(\sqrt{y}\) and
  • for each \(y\) in the range \(1\le y\le 4\text{,}\) \(x\) runs from \(y-2\) to \(\sqrt{y}\text{.}\)
