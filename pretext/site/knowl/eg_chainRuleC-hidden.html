Define \(w(x,t)=f(x+ct)\) and \(W(x,t)=\pdiff{w}{t}(x,t)=cf'(x+ct)=F\big(u(x,t)\big)\) where \(F(u)=cf'(u)\) and \(u(x,t)=x+ct\text{.}\) Then

\begin{align*} \frac{\partial^2}{\partial t^2}f(x+ct) &=\pdiff{W}{t}(x,t) =\diff{F}{u}\big(u(x,t)\big) \pdiff{u}{t}(x,t) =c\,f''(x+ct)\,c\\ &=c^2\,f''(x+ct) \end{align*}
