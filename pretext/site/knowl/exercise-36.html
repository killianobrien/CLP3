Exercise 28.

Let \(\vp=\llt -1,4,2\rgt ,\ \vq=\llt 3,1,-1\rgt ,\ \vr=\llt 2,-3,-1\rgt \text{.}\) Check, by direct computation, that

  1. \(\displaystyle \vp\times\vp=\vZero\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \vp\times\vq=-\vq\times\vp\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \vp\times(3\vr)=3(\vp\times\vr)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \vp\times(\vq+\vr) = \vp\times\vq+\vp\times\vr\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \vp\times(\vq\times\vr) \ne (\vp\times\vq)\times\vr\)
