(a) On the domain of integration,

  • \(x\) runs from \(0\) to \(1\text{,}\) and
  • for each fixed \(x\) in that range, \(y\) runs from \(\sqrt{x}\) to \(1\text{.}\) We may rewrite \(y=\sqrt{x}\) as \(x=y^2\text{,}\) which is a rightward opening parabola.
