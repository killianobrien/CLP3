Exercise Group 3.1.7.1–2.

Exercises — Stage 1

1.

For each of the following, evaluate the given double integral without using iteration. Instead, interpret the integral as, for example, an area or a volume.

  1. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_{-1}^3\int_{-4}^1 \dee{y}\,\dee{x}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_0^2\int_0^{\sqrt{4-y^2}} \dee{x}\,\dee{y}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_{-3}^3\int_0^{\sqrt{9-y^2}}\sqrt{9-x^2-y^2}\ \dee{x}\,\dee{y}\)
2.

Let \(f(x,y)= 12 x^2y^3\text{.}\) Evaluate

  1. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_0^3 f(x,y)\,\dee{x}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_0^2 f(x,y)\,\dee{y}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_0^2\int_0^3 f(x,y)\,\dee{x}\,\dee{y}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_0^3\int_0^2 f(x,y)\,\dee{y}\,\dee{x}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \ds\int_0^3\int_0^2 f(x,y)\,\dee{x}\,\dee{y}\)
