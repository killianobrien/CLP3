The Laplace transform, \(F(s)\) makes sense as a complex valued function of the complex variable \(s\text{.}\) However we shall only consider it mostly as a function of the real variable \(s\text{.}\) In fact we shall not be making any particular use of the individual values of \(F\text{,}\) rather we shall be exploiting the existence of these transforms and their associated properties in order to find solutions to certain ODEs.
