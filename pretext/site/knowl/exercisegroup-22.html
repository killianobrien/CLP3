Exercise Group 2.1.2.6–8.

Exercises — Stage 2

6.

Evaluate, if possible,

  1. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow(2,-1)}\ \big(xy+x^2\big)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow(0,0)}\ \frac{x}{x^2+y^2}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow(0,0)}\ \frac{x^2}{x^2+y^2}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow(0,0)}\ \frac{x^3}{x^2+y^2}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow(0,0)}\ \frac{x^2y^2}{x^2+y^4}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow (0,0)}\ \frac{(\sin x)\left(e^y-1\right)}{xy}\)
7. ✳.
  1. Find the limit: \(\ds \lim_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{x^8+y^8}{x^4+y^4}\text{.}\)
  2. Prove that the following limit does not exist: \(\ds \lim_{(x,y)\to(0,0)}\frac{xy^5}{x^8+y^{10}}\text{.}\)
8. ✳.

Evaluate each of the following limits or show that it does not exist.

  1. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow (0,0)}\frac{x^3-y^3}{x^2+y^2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow (0,0)}\frac{x^2-y^4}{x^2+y^4}\)
