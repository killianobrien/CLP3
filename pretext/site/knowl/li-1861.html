To get the mass of any one beam, say the beam whose \(y\) coordinate runs from \(y\) to \(y+\dee{y}\text{,}\) we just add up the masses of the approximate cubes in that beam, by integrating \(x\) from its smallest value on the beam, namely \(0\text{,}\) to its largest value on the beam, namely \(\sqrt{1-y^2-z^2}\text{.}\) The mass of the beam is thus
\begin{gather*} \dee{y}\,\dee{z} \int_0^{\sqrt{1-y^2-z^2}} \dee{x}\,xyz \end{gather*}
