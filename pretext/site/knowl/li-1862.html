To get the mass of any one plate, say the plate whose \(z\) coordinate runs from \(z\) to \(z+\dee{z}\text{,}\) we just add up the masses of the beams in that plate, by integrating \(y\) from its smallest value on the plate, namely \(0\text{,}\) to its largest value on the plate, namely \(\sqrt{1-z^2}\text{.}\) The mass of the plate is thus
\begin{gather*} \dee{z}\int_0^{\sqrt{1-z^2}}\dee{y}\int_0^{\sqrt{1-y^2-z^2}} \dee{x}\,xyz \end{gather*}
