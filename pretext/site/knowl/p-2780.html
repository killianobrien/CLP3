\begin{align*} \dblInt_\cR f\big(x,y\big)\,\dee{x}\,\dee{y} &= \int_a^b \left[\int_{B(x)}^{T(x)} f\big(x,y\big)\,\dee{y}\right]\dee{x}\\ &=\int_a^b \int_{B(x)}^{T(x)} f\big(x,y\big)\,\dee{y}\,\dee{x} =\int_a^b \dee{x} \int_{B(x)}^{T(x)} \dee{y}\, f\big(x,y\big) \end{align*}

The last three integrals here are called iterated integrals, for obvious reasons.
