7.

Let \(\al \gt 0\) be a constant. Show that \(\displaystyle u(x,y,z,t) =\frac{1}{t^{3/2}} e^{-(x^2+y^2+z^2)/(4\al t)}\) satisfies the heat equation

\begin{equation*} u_t = \al\big(u_{xx} + u_{yy} + u_{zz} \big) \end{equation*}

for all \(t \gt 0\)
