The volume in the first octant is thus
\begin{align*} \text{Volume}(\cV_1) &=\int_0^{\pi/2}\dee{\theta}\,\int_0^\beta \dee{\varphi}\ \sin\varphi \int_0^a\dee{\rho}\ \rho^2\\ &=\frac{a^3}{3}\int_0^{\pi/2}\dee{\theta}\, \int_0^\beta \dee{\varphi}\ \sin\varphi\\ &=\frac{a^3}{3}\big[1-\cos\be\big]\int_0^{\pi/2}\dee{\theta}\\ &=\frac{\pi a^3}{6}\big[1-\cos\be\big] \end{align*}
