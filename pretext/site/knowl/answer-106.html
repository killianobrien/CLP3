Answer 1.6.2.11.1.

(a)

\begin{align*} \vv(t)&= -a \sin t\,\hi+a\cos t\,\hj+c\,\hk\\ \diff{s}{t}(t)&= \sqrt{a^2+c^2}\\ \va(t)&= -a \cos t\,\hi-a\sin t\,\hj \end{align*}

The path is a helix with radius \(a\) and with each turn having height \(2\pi c\text{.}\)

(b)

\begin{align*} \vv(t)&= a \cos 2t\,\hi +a\sin 2t\,\hj-a\sin t\,\hk\\ \diff{s}{t}(t)&= a\sqrt{1+\sin^2t}\\ \va(t)&= -2a \sin 2t\,\hi +2a\cos 2t\,\hj-a\cos t\,\hk \end{align*}

The \((x,y)\) coordinates go around a circle of radius \(\frac{a}{2}\) and centre \(\big(0,\frac{a}{2}\big)\) counterclockwise. At the same time the \(z\) coordinate oscillates over the interval between \(1\) and \(-1\) half as fast.
