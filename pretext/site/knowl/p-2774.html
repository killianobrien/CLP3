In your previous calculus courses you defined and worked with single variable integrals, like \(\int_a^b f(x)\ \dee{x}\text{.}\) In this chapter, we define and work with multivariable integrals, like \(\dblInt_R f(x,y)\ \dee{x}\,\dee{y}\) and \(\tripInt_V f(x,y,z)\ \dee{x}\,\dee{y}\,\dee{z}\text{.}\) We start with two variable integrals.
