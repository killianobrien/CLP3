(c) The region is

\begin{equation*} V = \Set{(x,y,z)}{x^2+y^2\le z\le 2y} \end{equation*}

There are \(z\)'s that obey \(x^2+y^2\le z\le 2y\) if and only if

\begin{align*} x^2+y^2\le 2y &\iff x^2+y^2-2y \le 0 \iff x^2 + (y-1)^2 \le 1 \end{align*}
