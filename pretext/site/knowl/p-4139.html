Solutions to ODEs will often feature a number of undetermined constants which can be determined if extra information is given in form of boundary conditions, i.e. known values of the solution \(y\) at certain points in the domain, or initial conditions, i.e. known values of the solution \(y(t)\) and some of its derivatives at \(t=0\text{.}\) Such initial values usually come from knowing the initial state of a system, the future behaviour of which will be governed by the ODE.
