The cartesian and cylindrical coordinates are related by

\begin{align*} x&=\rho\cos\phi & y&=\rho\sin\phi & z&=z\\ \rho&=\sqrt{x^2+y^2} & \phi&=\arctan\frac{y}{x} & z&=z \end{align*}

Here are three figures showing a surface of constant \(\rho\text{,}\) a surface of constant \(\phi\text{,}\) and a surface of constant \(z\text{.}\)
