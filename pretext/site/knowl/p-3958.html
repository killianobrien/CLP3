Consider the top half of a ball of radius 2 centred at the origin. Suppose that the ball has variable density equal to \(9z\) units of mass per unit volume.

  1. Set up a triple integral giving the mass of this half-ball.
  2. Find out what fraction of that mass lies inside the cone
    \begin{equation*} z=\sqrt{x^2+y^2} \end{equation*}
