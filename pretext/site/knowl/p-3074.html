In the given integral

  • \(x\) runs from \(-2\) to \(2\) and
  • for each fixed \(x\) between \(-2\) and \(2\text{,}\) \(y\) runs from \(x^2\) to \(4\)

So the domain of integration is

\begin{equation*} D = \Set{(x,y)}{-2\le x\le 2,\ x^2\le y\le 4} \end{equation*}
