(a) Call the solid \(V\text{.}\) In cylindrical coordinates

  • \(x^2+y^2+z^2\le 2\) is \(r^2+z^2\le 2\) and
  • \(\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\le z\) is \(r\le z\) and
  • the density \(\de=r^2\text{,}\) and
  • \(\dee{V}\) is \(r\,\dee{r}\,\dee{\theta}\,\dee{z}\)
