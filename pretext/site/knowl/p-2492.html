  1. Find all critical points of \(f\text{,}\) and classify each one as a local maximum, a local minimum, or saddle point.
  2. Find the location and value of the absolute maximum and minimum of \(f\) on the triangular region \(x \ge 0\text{,}\) \(y \ge 0\text{,}\) \(x + y \le 8\text{.}\)
