We now use a little trickery to construct a vector perpendicular to the surface \(G(x,y,z)=0\) at the point \(\big(x_0\,,\,y_0\,,\,z_0\big)\text{.}\) Imagine that you are walking on the surface and that at time \(0\) you are at the point \(\big(x_0\,,\,y_0\,,\,z_0\big)\text{.}\) Let \(\vr(t) =\big(x(t)\,,\,y(t)\,,\,z(t)\big)\) denote your position at time \(t\text{.}\)
