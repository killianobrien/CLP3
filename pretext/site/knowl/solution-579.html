Solution 4.2.3.1.
\begin{align*} \L{f(t)} \amp = \trans{t},\\ \amp = \int_0^\infty t \, \frac{d}{dt} \left ( \frac{e^{-st}}{-s} \right ) \, dt,\\ \amp = \left [ t\, \frac{e^{-st}}{-s} \right ]_0^\infty - \int_0^\infty \frac{e^{-st}}{-s} \, dt , \text{ int. by parts},\\ \amp = \frac{1}{s} \int_0^\infty e^{-st} \, dt, \text{ for $s>0$},\\ \amp = \frac{1}{s^2}. \end{align*}
