(a) In cylindrical coordinates

  • \(x^2+y^2+z^2\le 1\) is \(r^2+z^2\le 1\) and
  • \(x^2+y^2\le z^2\) is \(r^2\le z^2\) and
  • \(\dee{V}\) is \(r\,\dee{r}\,\dee{\theta}\,\dee{z}\)
