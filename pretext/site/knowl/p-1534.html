Let \(w(s,t) = u(2s + 3t, 3s - 2t)\) for some twice differentiable function \(u = u(x, y)\text{.}\)

  1. Find \(w_{ss}\) in terms of \(u_{xx}\text{,}\) \(u_{xy}\) , and \(u_{yy}\text{.}\) You can assume that \(u_{xy} = u_{yx}\text{.}\)
  2. Suppose \(u_{xx} + u_{yy} = 0\text{.}\) For what constant \(A\) will \(w_{ss} = Aw_{tt}\text{?}\)
