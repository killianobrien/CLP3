(a) In the given integral

  • \(x\) runs from \(0\) to \(1\) and
  • for each fixed \(x\) between \(0\) and \(1\text{,}\) \(y\) runs from \(x\) to \(1\)

So the domain of integration is

\begin{equation*} D = \Set{(x,y)}{0\le x\le 1,\ x\le y\le 1} \end{equation*}
