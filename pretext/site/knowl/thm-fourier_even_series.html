Theorem 1.4.5. Fourier series of even functions.

Suppose that \(g\) is an even function with a Fourier series. Then its series takes the form

\begin{equation*} g(x) = \frac{1}{2} a_0 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n \cos(nx), \end{equation*}

i.e. the sine Fourier coefficients are all zero. Moreover the \(a_n\) can be given by the formula

\begin{equation*} a_n = \frac{2}{\pi} \int_0^\pi g(x) \cos(nx) . \end{equation*}
